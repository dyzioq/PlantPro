About the Aplication

See how you can improve your farming business with PlantPro system.

Start by choosing a job

Employees start a day with picking up a job that they will do. If you decide to user our software, we will adjust the list to your farm needs.

Different types of field

Our clients often have large fields in their farm - that’s why the next step for your worker is to pick a field that they will work on. This way your business is becoming more efficient because work is split equally between employees.
